INFOGRAPHICS

I started working for Friends of the American University of Afghanistan (FAUAF) in July of 2021.

In August of 2021, when the Afghan government collapsed and the American University of Afghanistan (AUAF) was forced to evacuate its campus, many thought the university would fall alongside the government. However, AUAF prevailed. The following semester, AUAF received more applications than ever before and continued educating young Afghan men and women online and at their new hub in Education City in Doha, Qatar.

Eager to share the university's impressive accomplishments, I created a digestible infographic card that both updated donors on AUAF's status and highlighted the incredible resilience of the students, faculty, and staff. Since then, I have created and produced an infographic card for each semester. These 4" x 6" cards are shared physically and digitally with current, prospective, and lapsed donors and have been invaluable for raising funds and awareness.
